The Problem


Australia has one of the highest rates of food allergies in the world. Accordingly, nuts and dairy allergies top the chart with increase in cases amongst children living in Victoria.

The Solution


Five Nuts focuses on raising awareness on food allergies and provide preventative measures including medical knowledge and first aid methods for Victorian residents. This helps the people to appreciate the severity of the problem and understand the potential health risks associated with it.

3.2 million Australians suffer from food allergies

Approximately 26.4% proportion of children aged 2-19 years avoid food due to allergy or intolerance.

17% of Victorians suffer from food allergy

Fun Fact

What is Food Allergy?

An adverse reaction by the immune system towards a certain type of food is termed as Food Allergy. For many sensitive children, minute allergic reaction can be frightening and life-threatening. In extreme cases it can lead to immediate hospitalisation or even death.

8 Major Food Allergens


Egg, Dairy Products, Peanut, Tree Nuts, Sesame, Soy, Wheat, Fish and other Seafoods.

Triggers


1. Food Intolerance towards specific type of food
2. Genetic issues
3. Environmental Impact by moving to a different country
